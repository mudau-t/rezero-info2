About This Website

Re:Zero Info is a fan-made informational website created for fans of Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World. The goal of this site is to provide clear, simple, and organized information about the series in one easy place.

This website focuses on characters, story details, helpful links, and community support resources. It is designed to be easy to read, mobile-friendly, and accessible to both new and long-time fans.

Re:Zero Info is not an official website and is not affiliated with the creators, publishers, or studios behind Re:Zero. All content is shared for fan and informational purposes only.

This project exists to support the community and help fans learn more about the Re:Zero universe.
